Top 3 Movies - 2025

This is a short list of the best movies I have watched this year

Totally Killer

2023 | Horror/Comedy | 1h46m | English

Kiernan Shipka | Olivia Holt | Julie Bowen

This is a pretty fun murder mystery + sci-fi movie involving serial killers, time travel and Halloween.
Might just be the perfect combination


Court

2025 | Drama | 2h35m | Telugu

Priyadarshi | Sridevi Appala | Harsh Rohan

This is an intense courtroom drama depicting the story of how communities exploit the POCSO act to separate young lovers over differences in caste


The Life List

2025 | Romance/Comedy | 2h5m | English

Sofia Carson | Kyle Allen | Connie Britton

This super cute rom-com is the story of a once ambitious girl that lost her spark somewhere in the trenches of the real world until her rich, late-mother inspires her to go ahead and live her life to the fullest in order to receive her inheritance, all based on a "life list" the girl wrote as a teen.
